Jack's Favorite Movies/Movie Series

Top 3 Movies/Series

Star Wars

Star Wars has been a big thing in my family for my whole life. My mother loved it as a child and got my and my brother into it too. My favorite of the series is A New Hope but I like all of the orignial and prequel trilogies. I'm not as big a fan of the sequels but they have their moments too.

The Muppet Movie

I was always a fan of the Muppets so when I discovered the original Muppet's Movie I was naturally excited. The combination of familiar characters and a witty humour style make it an easy movie to go back and watch again.

The Incredibles

Super Hero's were never that interesting, but that wasn't the point of the incredibles. The high quality comedy, hooking mystery, high stakes actions, and just a dash of nostalgia make this one of my favorite movies of all time.
